Student Projects

Semester, Bachelor and Master Theses

We are working on a large variety of topics in the field of computer graphics and related areas: Physics-based animation, rendering, geometric modeling, computational materials, computer-aided learning, medical simulations, display technology, as well as image and video-based techniques. Check our project pages at CGL and Disney Research for more information.

If you are looking for a semester, bachelor or master thesis in our lab, take a look at our current offers listed below. Although we try to keep this page up-to-date, there might be other projects available. Therefore, if you have an interest in computer graphics and you are searching for a project, just come and talk to us.

In case you are interested in a project, or if you have own ideas, just drop by, we are always looking for interested students. To schedule a first meeting where we can discuss relevant projects, please contact the thesis coordinator (cgl-thesis@inf.ethz.ch).

Remarks

It is still the case that grades have to be formally approved in the "Notenkonferenz" (grade conference) of the department. This implies that master students have to start before a specific date if they want to receive the master degree directly. If they miss one of these dates they have to wait 6 months for the degree after completion of the thesis. However, a receipt of successful completion of the master degree is given by the department if they start later (which is needed for job applications).
